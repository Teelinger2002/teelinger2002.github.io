Design philosophy

How I work.

Framework
Old, New, Borrowed, Blue

Every design challenge requires balancing legacy equities (old), new capabilities (new), external forces (borrowed), and go-to-market strategy (blue). A framework that applies to brand systems and agentic platforms equally.

Capability
IC = Manager

Hire me for one, get the other for free. I cannot lead design without mentoring. I cannot manage a team without getting my hands in the work. Director-level strategy and principal-level execution are not separate modes — they're simultaneous.

Methodology
North Star + Crawl-Walk-Run

Vision without roadmap is decoration. I establish where we're going, then break it into foundational investments that build progressively. Each phase is validated before the next begins. Outcomes-first throughout.

AI Principle
Preserve Human Agency

AI that excludes humans from meaningful participation in outcomes is a design failure. My charter: preserve the user's ability to demonstrate competence, inject judgment, and maintain psychological ownership — in every AI system I build.
